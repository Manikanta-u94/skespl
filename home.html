Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main focus of Sri Kalash Edu Skills Pvt. Ltd.?
Sri Kalash Edu Skills Pvt. Ltd. focuses on providing advanced computer skills training to high school and college students, unemployed youth, and in-service employees. We aim to bridge the skill gap and enhance employability through innovative and cost-effective IT training programs.
What types of training programs do you offer?
We offer a variety of training programs, including low-cost paid training and no-cost training utilizing government initiatives. Our programs cover various aspects of IT, including software development, networking, and employable and self-employable skills.
How does Sri Kalash ensure the quality and reliability of its software development services?
Our software development wing uses state-of-the-art infrastructure, advanced equipment, and the latest productivity tools to ensure high-quality services. We conduct regular walk-throughs to ensure functionality, reliability, and maintainability of the software we develop, whether it's for Local Area Networks or Enterprise-Wide Networks.
What are the key areas of expertise for Sri Kalash Edu Skills Pvt. Ltd.?
Our expertise lies in delivering quality IT solutions, including customized software development, networking, and IT infrastructure management. We have strong partnerships with leading technology providers, ensuring that we can offer the most suitable and cost-effective solutions to our clients.
What makes Sri Kalash Edu Skills Pvt. Ltd. different from other IT training providers?
We emphasize quality, affordability, and innovation in our training programs. Our mission is to provide efficient skill training through the latest technology, ensuring sustainable livelihood for the unskilled and unemployed youth. Our commitment to customer satisfaction and employee motivation drives us to achieve excellence in every operation.
