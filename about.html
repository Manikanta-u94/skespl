Company Background

  •   Established Sri Sai Computers Systems in Vizianagaram in 1998 to impart advanced computer skills to high school and college students, unemployed youth, and in-service employees.
  •   Mr. B.K. Sharma, Director, took a franchisee of Datapro Computers Pvt. Ltd. and trained 200 to 300 candidates annually.
  •   Executed the prestigious 5-year 'Deepthi' Computer Education program for Tribal Welfare Educational Institutions in the Parvathipuram ITDA area of Vizianagaram District from 2001-2006, the first of its kind.
  •   Expanded the 'Deepthi' program to almost all ITDAs in the combined A.P State.
  •   Later, established Datha Sai Computers in Vizianagaram, gaining a strong reputation for computer skilling activities.
  •   Mr. B.K. Sharma was appointed to the Management Board of Datapro Computers Pvt. Ltd., helping Datapro reach new heights in business.
  •   In 2009, imparted computer education in 1154 schools in the combined State of Andhra Pradesh as part of the '5000 Schools Program' launch.
  •   In 2017-18, registered as Sri Kalash Edu Skills Pvt. Ltd. to expand computer education programs nationwide under Mr. B.K. Sharma's leadership.
  •   Today, Sri Kalash Edu Skills is a mature and fast-growing company, committed to providing reliable and cost-effective IT solutions across India.
  •   Emphasis on quality, top human resources, and cutting-edge solutions drive its commitment to excellence in software skills, networking expertise, project management, and IT domain knowledge.
